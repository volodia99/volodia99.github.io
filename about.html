About me

I'm a PosDoc at the Institute for Planetary sciences and Astrophysics (IPAG), in Grenoble. I am currently working on planetary evolution in protoplanetary disks.

Research experience

  1. 2015

    IAS, Orsay

    Internship (1.5 months) about the magnetic activity of the Sun, in visible and in extreme UV regions. Under the supervision of Frédéric Baudin.

  2. 2016

    MSI, Montreal

    Internship (3 months) about a comparison of two black hole spin estimators for active galactic nuclei. Under the supervision of Daryl Haggard & Daniel Capellupo.

  3. 2017

    IAP, Paris

    Master's thesis (3 months) about the life cycle of black holes in galaxies. Under the supervision of Marta Volonteri & Alexander Y. Wagner.

  4. 2018

    IRAP, Toulouse

    PhD (3 years) about observational predictions of planetary migration in the dust content of protoplanetary disks. Under the supervision of Clément Baruteau.

  5. 2021

    IPAG, Grenoble

    Postdoctorate (4 years) about the interactions between protoplanetary disks, magnetic winds and planets. Under the supervision of Geoffroy Lesur.
